Why Ainjibi.com?

Ainjibi.com is a product discovery and consumer driven review platform. Millions of Ainjibi.com users share their product and service experience with brands. Here, brands have an opportunity to peep into the minds of their current and potential consumers and build upon their existing products to retain more customers and increase their sales

How Ainjibi for Brands work?

User Purchase
Customer buys your product or service
MouthShut Invites
MouthShut invites all your customers to share their experience through Verified Invitations
User Reviews
Your customers visit your product on MouthShut.com and post their true & genuine experience
You Connect with Users
You get a chance to connect with all happy/unhappy customers and increase retention
User Recommends
Let your customers recommend your product/brand by sharing their valuable feedback
